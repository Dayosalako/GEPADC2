Thematic Areas

GBV Protection

Providing comprehensive protection and response services for Gender-Based Violence survivors in conflict-affected regions.

Peacebuilding

Promoting Women, Peace, and Security (WPS) through advocacy and community engagement for social cohesion.

Education

Increasing access to quality education for girls and promoting educational rights in marginalized communities.

Livelihood

Socio-economic empowerment initiatives to help women achieve financial independence and stability.

Vision Blueprint

Strategy
Twenty
Thirty.

From emergency intervention to institutional permanence. Our 2030 roadmap is designed to weave gender equality and peacebuilding into the very fabric of Nigerian governance.

Strategic
Framework

Pillar Priority Focus 2030 Target
Governance Gender-responsive policy reform & state-level advocacy. Protection laws active in all 36 states.
Infrastructure WASH Resilience & Climate-smart sanitation facilities. 500+ solar-powered water points in camps.
Human Capital Vocational scaling & financial literacy for survivors. 100,000 women trained in trade skills.
Security Community-led mediation & peace-building units. 40% reduction in local conflict incidents.
01

Phase One: Data

2025 - 2026. Focused on mapping protection gaps in underserved border regions and digitizing field reporting.

02

Phase Two: Scale

2027 - 2028. Scaling our Field Expert network and establishing the GEPaDC Regional Training Hub in Abuja.

03

Phase Three: Legacy

2029 - 2030. Full handover of community-led initiatives to local governance and publishing the Decade of Impact paper.
