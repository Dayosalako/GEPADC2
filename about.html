ORGANISATIONAL
BACKGROUND

Gender Equality Peace and Development Centre (GEPaDC) is a non-governmental, nonpolitical, and non-religious organization. The organization was established in 2008 and was registegreen with the Nigeria Corporate Affairs Commission in 2010 with registration number CAC/IT/39885.

Our Vision

"A society where women and men, girls and boys realize their full potential and participate in every sphere on equal basis and where the rights of everyone including right to a life of dignity are recognized and respected irrespective of sex and disability."

Our Mission

To work for gender equality, empowerment and advancement of women and girls, peace and sustainable development To this end, we focus our efforts on promoting the social, educational, economical, and political development of women and girl’s including advocating for women’s rights, eliminating discrimination against women including genderbased violence (GBV), advancing women’s capacities and leadership skills, promoting their participation in the decisions that affect their lives, and amplifying their voices for peace and security at all levels..

Core Values
