Privacy
Protocol.

01. Data Collection

GEPaDC collects personal data only when strictly necessary for humanitarian assistance. This includes identity information for protection services and contact details for volunteers. We adhere to the "Do No Harm" principle in all data handling.

02. Information Usage

Survivor data is strictly confidential. We do not share personal identifiers with third parties or donors without explicit, informed consent, except where required by Nigerian law for safety reasons.

03. Rights of Data Subjects

Individuals have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of their personal information from our field databases at any time.
