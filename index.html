Institutional Profile

Founded 2008.
Rooted in Resilience.

Established in 2008, Gender Equality, Peace and Development Centre (GEPaDC) focuses on the most vulnerable conflict-affected regions of Nigeria. With active offices in Borno, Adamawa, and Yobe, we specialize in high-impact humanitarian response and long-term gender justice.

Who We Are

Rooted in Equality.
Driven by Peace.

Founded in 2008, GEPaDC operates at the intersection of gender justice and humanitarian response. Based in Nigeria's conflict-affected regions—with active field offices in Borno, Adamawa, and Yobe—we provide life-saving protection and sustainable development for women, girls, and vulnerable populations.
